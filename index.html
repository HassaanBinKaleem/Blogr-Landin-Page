Introducing an extensible editor

Blogr features an exceedingly intuitive interface which lets you focus on one thing: creating content. Flexible content management enables uses to easily move through posts. increases the usability of your blog by adding customized categories, sections, format, or flow. With this functionality, you're in full control.
